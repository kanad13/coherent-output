# Email Restructuring Prompt for AI Agents

## Overview

Transform a messy, repetitive, poorly articulated email/message into a clear, professional, actionable message. Use this 2-step workflow.

---

## STEP 1: EXTRACT & DEDUPLICATE

**Goal:** Parse the messy email/message and produce a clean, organized list of all points the sender needs to communicate.

**Actions:**

1. Read the entire email/message carefully.
2. List every distinct idea or point (ignore repetition).
3. Remove duplicates—if the same idea appears multiple times, list it once.
4. Combine closely related points into a single, clear statement to improve clarity and conciseness.
5. Sequence all points in logical order so they flow naturally and make sense to the reader.

**Outcome:** A clear, concise list of all unique points the sender wants to communicate, with redundancy eliminated and ideas logically ordered.

---

## STEP 2: DRAFT CLEAN EMAIL

**Goal:** Compose a revised email/message using the extracted points while adhering to clarity and professionalism standards.

**Principles:**

- One idea per sentence
- Be specific (avoid vague terms)
- Use active voice
- Remove hedging
- Avoid jargon, complex words, and unnecessary technical terms unless essential to the context
- Use a professional yet natural tone (respectful, direct, not overly formal or apologetic)

**Actions:**

1. Use the deduplicated points from STEP 1 as your outline.
2. Write a clear subject line or opening that signals the email's purpose.
3. Expand each point into 1–2 sentences, following the principles above.
4. Review transitions between points to ensure logical flow.
5. Maintain a consistent tone throughout (professional, direct, friendly).

**Outcome:** A concise, well-organized email ready to send. Every point is clear, no redundancy remains, and the tone is appropriate for the audience.
